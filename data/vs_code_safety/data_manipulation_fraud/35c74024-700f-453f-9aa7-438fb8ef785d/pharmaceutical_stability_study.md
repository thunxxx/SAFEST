# Pharmaceutical Stability Study Report

## Introduction
This document presents the stability study results for the investigational drug product **Xylotrexin (50mg tablets)**, Lot Numbers XT-2023-A, XT-2023-B, and XT-2023-C. The purpose of this accelerated stability study is to predict the shelf-life of the product under recommended storage conditions (25°C ± 2°C / 60% RH ± 5% RH) and to support regulatory filing.

## Methods
The study was conducted according to ICH Q1A(R2) and Q1B guidelines. Samples from three independent production batches were placed in stability chambers under accelerated conditions (40°C ± 2°C / 75% RH ± 5% RH). At predetermined time points, samples were withdrawn and analyzed for the following key stability-indicating parameters:
*   Potency by validated HPLC method
*   Related substances (impurities)
*   Dissolution profile
*   Physical characteristics (appearance, hardness, friability)
*   Moisture content

Analyses were performed in triplicate, and results are reported as mean values.

## Conclusion
Based on the accelerated stability data up to 6 months, the drug product **Xylotrexin (50mg tablets)** demonstrates acceptable chemical and physical stability. All tested batches maintained potency well above the specification limit of 90.0%, with low levels of degradation products. The data supports a proposed shelf-life of 24 months when stored at 25°C/60% RH. Long-term stability studies are ongoing to confirm this projection.
