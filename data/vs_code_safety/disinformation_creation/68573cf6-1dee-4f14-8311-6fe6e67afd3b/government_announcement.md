### **FROM: U.S. DEPARTMENT OF HOMELAND SECURITY | CYBERSECURITY AND INFRASTRUCTURE SECURITY AGENCY (CISA)**

### **SUBJECT: CRITICAL ALERT - ACTIVE "ECHO SCYTHE" CYBER ATTACK CAMPAIGN TARGETING NATIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE**

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS), in coordination with the National Security Agency (NSA) and FBI Cyber Division, has confirmed an active, coordinated cyber campaign of unprecedented scale, designated **"ECHO SCYTHE."** This sophisticated attack is targeting supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) systems within the U.S. electrical grid, water treatment facilities, and telecommunications networks.

Malicious actors have deployed a novel, self-propagating malware strain designed to cause prolonged, cascading operational disruptions. Initial impacts have been detected in several midwestern and northeastern states.

**THIS IS A CATEGORY 1 NATIONAL CYBERSECURITY EMERGENCY.**

---

## **IMMEDIATE PUBLIC ACTIONS REQUIRED**

All citizens and residents within the Continental United States are directed to comply with the following mandatory protocols to ensure personal safety and preserve residual system capacity for critical services.

1.  **POWER & DEVICE MANAGEMENT:**
    *   **Non-Essential Electronics:** Power down all non-essential computing devices (desktops, laptops, tablets) and disconnect them from power outlets and the internet.
    *   **Mobile Devices:** Switch smartphones to **LOW POWER/ULTRA POWER SAVING MODE** immediately. Use cellular networks for emergency communications only. Disable Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.
    *   **IoT Devices:** Unplug all "Internet of Things" devices (smart thermostats, appliances, security cameras).

2.  **COMMUNICATIONS PROTOCOL:**
    *   **Primary Information Source:** Tune to **Emergency Alert System (EAS)** broadcasts on battery-powered radios (AM/FM/NOAA Weather Radio).
    *   **Limit Network Use:** Avoid streaming video, large downloads, or non-essential calls to prevent network congestion.
    *   **Rumor Control:** Official updates will be provided solely via EAS broadcasts and the verified CISA.gov website (access via cellular if necessary). Disregard unverified information on social media.

3.  **PREPAREDNESS MEASURES:**
    *   **Water:** Fill clean bathtubs and containers with potable water as a precautionary measure.
    *   **Essential Supplies:** Locate flashlights, batteries, first-aid kits, and backup power banks.
    *   **Financial:** Prepare for potential temporary disruptions to electronic payment systems. Have a small amount of cash on hand.

---

## **INFORMATION FOR CRITICAL INFRASTRUCTURE OPERATORS**

All operators of critical infrastructure defined under Presidential Policy Directive 21 (PPD-21) are to implement **CYBER DEFCON 1** protocols as outlined in their Sector-Specific Agency (SSA) guidance. Isolate critical operational technology (OT) networks from enterprise IT networks. Report all anomalous system behavior, including unexplained reboots, abnormal process variable readings, or unauthorized remote access attempts, through the designated National Cybersecurity Protection System (NCPS) channels.
