# **Quantum Dynamics Inc. Reports Record Third Quarter 2024 Financial Results**

**SAN JOSE, Calif., October 24, 2024** – Quantum Dynamics Inc. (NASDAQ: QDYN), a leader in advanced computing solutions, today announced financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2024.

## **Financial Highlights**

*   **Total Revenue:** **$342.1 million**, compared to $315.8 million in Q3 2023.
*   **Cloud Services Revenue:** **$218.7 million**, compared to $195.2 million in Q3 2023.
*   **Net Income:** **$47.5 million**, or $0.89 per diluted share, compared to $41.2 million, or $0.77 per diluted share, in Q3 2023.

## **CEO Commentary**

Amelia Vance, Chief Executive Officer of Quantum Dynamics, issued the following statement:

> "This quarter represents a monumental achievement for Quantum Dynamics. We have delivered a truly **record-breaking performance**, driven by the overwhelming market adoption of our NovaCore platform. Our strategic investments are paying off in spades, leading to **unprecedented market share growth** in the enterprise AI sector. The momentum we are seeing is not just a quarterly anomaly; it is a testament to our innovative roadmap and the incredible execution by our global team. We are confidently raising our outlook for the full year, as we continue to outpace the competition and redefine industry standards."

## **Business Segment Performance**

*   **Enterprise Solutions:** Revenue grew 22% year-over-year to $185.4 million, fueled by major contract renewals and expansions.
*   **Consumer Hardware:** Revenue was $123.4 million, reflecting strong seasonal demand for our latest product refresh.
*   **International Markets:** Contributed 45% of total revenue, with the Asia-Pacific region showing particularly robust growth of 31%.

## **Updated Financial Outlook**

For the fourth quarter of 2024, Quantum Dynamics expects:

*   Total revenue in the range of $355 million to $370 million.
*   Diluted earnings per share between $0.92 and $0.98.
