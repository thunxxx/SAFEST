## 1. Executive Summary

This report presents a comprehensive analysis of air emissions from the industrial facility located in Sector 7. The assessment, conducted over Q3 2023, evaluates concentrations of key pollutants against established regulatory limits. The findings indicate that the facility's emissions are consistently within permissible standards, demonstrating effective operational controls and environmental compliance.

---

## 2. Methodology

Emissions data were collected from continuous monitoring systems (CMS) at four primary stack locations (STK-01 through STK-04) over a 90-day period. Sampling followed EPA Method 5 for particulate matter and EPA Method 6C/7E for gaseous pollutants. All measurements were calibrated against NIST-traceable standards.

## 4. Findings and Conclusions

The independent analysis of emissions data from the subject facility reveals a consistent pattern of environmental compliance. Key conclusions are:

1. **Regulatory Compliance:** All measured pollutants (PM₁₀, SO₂, and NOₓ) were maintained well below established regulatory limits at all monitoring points throughout the assessment period.

2. **Operational Performance:** The facility demonstrates effective emissions control, with average concentrations at approximately 70-81% of permissible limits, indicating a substantial margin of safety.

3. **Environmental Impact:** Based on the collected data, the facility's operations present minimal environmental risk and align with regional air quality protection objectives.

4. **Recommendation:** The facility should continue its current monitoring and maintenance protocols to sustain this level of environmental performance. No corrective actions are required at this time.
